Privacy Policy
Last Updated: April 2024
Introduction
PassArmor is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.
Section 01
Data Collection and Storage
Local Storage
All your passwords, usernames, and sensitive data are stored exclusively on your device's local memory.
No Cloud Servers
We do not own any servers, and we do not collect, store, or have access to your personal data.
Encryption
Your data is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256-GCM encryption.
Section 02
Password Leak Check Feature
k-Anonymity
When checking a password, the App generates a SHA-1 hash. Only the first 5 characters of this hash are sent to the "Have I Been Pwned" (HIBP) API.
Privacy Guaranteed
Your full password or full hash is never sent over the internet. The service provider cannot identify your password.
Section 03
Biometric Data
No Collection
We do not collect or store your fingerprints or face scans.
OS-Handled
Biometric authentication is handled entirely by the Android Operating System via Android's BiometricPrompt.
Section 04
Third-Party Services
The App only communicates with the "Have I Been Pwned" API for the leak check feature. No other third-party trackers or analytics are used.
Section 05
Your Consent
By using PassArmor, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
